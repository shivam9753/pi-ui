About Poems India

A place where writers who care about their craft find readers who care about good writing.

Why We Started

We started Poems India in 2018 because we noticed something: there were good writers scattered across social media who weren't getting the attention their work deserved.

People were reading the same few writers they already knew, missing out on voices they'd probably love if they could find them.

Since 2018

Connecting writers and readers

What We Do

Quality Publishing

We publish writing that feels real and thoughtful. Our submissions are open year-round because good work comes when it comes.

Live Events

We organize "The Stage is Yours" events where people can read their work aloud. Sometimes online, sometimes in cities across India.

Mosaic Newsletter

Our newsletter pulls everything together—poems we've published, thoughts on books and films, and other things we think are worth your time.

"There's something different about hearing poetry read by the person who wrote it."

What's Coming

Enhanced Platform

We're launching a new website that will make everything work better. Writers will be able to see where their submissions are in our process. Readers will find it easier to browse and discover poets they haven't read before.

Poetry Archive

We're building an archive of contemporary Indian poetry—a place where the poems being written now can be found by people reading years from now.

Why We Matter

There's good writing happening everywhere, but it's often hard to find. We try to be the place where writers who care about their craft can find readers who care about good writing.

Where people reading can trust they'll find something worth their time.

That's what we've been doing since 2018. That's what we'll keep doing.
