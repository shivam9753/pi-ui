General Rules

  • All submissions must be written in English.
  • Original work only. Submissions must be original and entirely unpublished.
  • Simultaneous submissions are fine, but please inform us immediately if your work is accepted elsewhere.
  • Please thoroughly proofread your work before sending it over.

Submission Formats & Limits

Poetry
Minimum of 3 poems
Prose
Minimum of 500 words
Articles, Essays, Cinema Essays & Book Reviews
Minimum of 1,000 words

Response Time

Our standard review window is up to 3 weeks.

Time-Sensitive Submissions: If your piece deals with timely topics or contemporary issues requiring urgent publication, please state so in your email. We review time-sensitive work on a priority basis.

Editorial Preference

What We Look For

We appreciate pieces that demonstrate depth of thought and clear artistic voice. For creative submissions we look for vivid imagery alongside a mastery of nuance. For prose and critical essays we welcome thoroughly researched arguments that bring refreshing perspectives to light. Your writing should feel purposeful and engaging from the very first line.

Red Flags to Avoid

Please avoid cliché themes or heavy reliance on overly complex language that obscures the core message. We do not accept submissions containing unverified factual claims or sloppy formatting. Plagiarism or AI generated content will lead to an immediate rejection. Ensure your arguments are focused rather than scattered or vague.

Rights & Legal

  • You always retain full copyright to your own work.
  • We ask for first digital publication rights. Our team will contact you directly before anything goes live.
  • You are free to withdraw your work anytime prior to official publication.

Have Questions?

Drop us a line at poemsindia@gmail.com. We generally reply to general inquiries within 2–3 business days.
