General Rules

  • Original work only. All submissions must be original and unpublished.
  • Simultaneous submissions are allowed. Please inform us if accepted elsewhere.
  • Please proofread your work before submitting.

Word Limits

Poetry
Up to 3 poems
Prose
No word limit
Articles & Cinema Essays
Min 1,000 words
Opinion
Min 500 words

Notes

  • Poetry: No line limit. Submit separate files for each poem when possible.
  • Prose: Creative nonfiction preferred. Keep readability in mind.
  • Articles & Cinema Essays: Citations required for factual claims.
  • Opinion: Contemporary issues only.

Response Time

  • Poetry & Prose: up to 3 weeks
  • Articles & Cinema Essays: around 1 week
  • Opinion: 24–48 hours (fast track)

Tracking Your Submission

You can track the status of your submission under “My Submissions” in your profile. All editorial communication happens via email.

Revisions

  • Revisions may be requested at the editor’s discretion.
  • Please respond within 72 hours.
  • Minor edits may be made for clarity and consistency.

Editorial Philosophy

Rights & Legal

  • You retain copyright to your work.
  • We request first publication rights. We will notify you before publishing.
  • You may withdraw your submission before publication.
