Submission Guidelines

Please read these guidelines before submitting your work

General Guidelines

• All submissions must be original work by the author

• Previously published work will not be accepted

• Please ensure your work is properly proofread before submission

• We accept simultaneous submissions but please notify us if your work is accepted elsewhere

• Response time is typically 4-6 weeks

Content-Specific Guidelines

Poetry

  • • Submit up to 5 poems per submission
  • • Any style or form welcome
  • • Maximum 100 lines per poem
  • • Include brief bio (50 words max)

Articles

  • • Word limit: 1,500-3,000 words
  • • Well-researched and fact-checked
  • • Include citations for sources
  • • Author bio (100 words max)

Cinema Essays

  • • Word limit: 2,000-4,000 words
  • • Focus on film analysis and critique
  • • Include filmography references
  • • Academic or journalistic style

Prose

  • • Word limit: 1,000-5,000 words
  • • Fiction or creative non-fiction
  • • Strong narrative voice
  • • Complete stories preferred

Submission Process

  1. 1 Complete the submission form with your work
  2. 2 Review your submission carefully before submitting
  3. 3 You'll receive a confirmation email within 24 hours
  4. 4 Our editorial team will review your submission
  5. 5 You'll hear back from us within 4-6 weeks

Rights & Legal

• By submitting, you confirm that you own the rights to your work

• You retain copyright to your work

• If accepted, we request first publication rights

• You may withdraw your submission at any time before publication

• We reserve the right to make minor editorial changes

What We're Looking For

  • • Authentic voices that offer fresh perspectives
  • • Strong craftsmanship with attention to language and structure
  • • Compelling narratives that engage readers emotionally
  • • Cultural diversity and underrepresented viewpoints
  • • Contemporary relevance with timeless appeal
  • • Risk-taking that pushes creative boundaries

Formatting Guidelines

• Use standard fonts (Times New Roman, Arial, or similar)

• Double-space prose submissions

• Single-space poetry with stanza breaks clearly indicated

• Include page numbers for multi-page submissions

• Use standard margins (1 inch on all sides)

• Save drafts regularly using our auto-save feature

Questions?

If you have any questions about our submission guidelines, please don't hesitate to contact us:

Email: submissionsXliteraryjournal.com
Response time: We typically respond to inquiries within 2-3 business days
